My Journey

  • UFLA 2024 - present
    Master's candidate in Computer Science
    Thesis: AI-Assisted Sensor Analysis for Detecting Meltdown Episodes in Adults with Autism
  • Unilavras 2022 - 2024
    Technological degree in Systems Analysis and Development
  • Floc.o Arquitetura 2019 - present
    Design and development of architectural projects, as well as implementation of the BIM methodology.
  • Prédios do Brasil 2019
    I traveled extensively through the North and Northeast regions of Brazil in order to capture the architectural diversity for the book Prédios do Brasil. During this project, I photographed emblematic and contemporary buildings, exploring the cultural and historical richness of these areas. The work involved detailed logistical planning, in-depth research into local architectural styles, interaction with communities and professionals in the field, as well as the application of advanced photography techniques. The result was a visual collection that highlights the plurality and uniqueness of Brazilian architecture, contributing to the appreciation and preservation of the country's architectural heritage.
  • GAM Arq. Associados 2019 - 2020
    As a member of Gam's real estate development team, I had the opportunity to work on truly innovative and sustainable projects. Specifically, I was involved in the design and development of 4 residential buildings in the state of Bahia, with a focus on sustainability and functionality. With a passion for design and a commitment to sustainability, I was able to contribute my skills and expertise to the Gam team.
  • Prédios de Salvador 2017 - Present
    The project consists of creating a comprehensive database and photographing the city's most emblematic architectural buildings. This venture aims to document Salvador's architectural diversity and richness, capturing everything from historic buildings to contemporary works. The database includes detailed information about each building, such as architectural style, date of construction, cultural relevance and historical context. Using advanced photography techniques, I seek to highlight the beauty and uniqueness of each building, contributing to the preservation and appreciation of the local architectural heritage.
  • Urban Arch.Studio 2009 - 2019
    As the founder and principal architect of Urban Recycle, I have acquired more than a decade of architectural experience and a strong commitment to sustainable design. My portfolio includes several award-winning projects, recognized at prestigious events such as the 9th São Paulo Biennale and two invitations to the Venice Biennale. At Urban Recycle, I oversaw all project creation and construction documentation, demonstrating proficiency in all aspects of architectural design and management. Client-focused, I was always about creating innovative and sustainable solutions tailored to clients' needs, ensuring that it was exceptional work that exceeded expectations. Leading a team of qualified, multinational and international professionals.
  • Unifacs 2004 - 2009
    Bachelor of Architecture and Urbanism
